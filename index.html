ABOUT US

Ever since Beta was founded in 1839, our mission has been dedicated to developing men of principle to lead a life of excellence. Having been established at UC San Diego in 2013, we have made it a priority to not only develop men to be leaders and successful students, but to help our brothers find a family away from home. Our tight knit brotherhood goes beyond simple friendship, providing each member a network they can give and recieve support from.

OUR VALUES

Without a defining set of characteristics that brings all of the brothers together, there would be no brotherhood. Our core values are not only the glue that binds us together, but they are traits that are instantly recognizable in every Beta around the country. In Beta, we have 5 core values: Trust, Responsible Conduct, Integrity, Intellectual Growth, and Mutual Assistance. All five act as a part of our identity, for without any one of the five the true mark of a Beta is lost.

Joining Beta gives you more than just a group of friends to hang out with, it gives you a brotherhood for life. Men who you can rely on to help you through hard times academically or in life. RUSH

Rush week for fraternities occurs during the second week of instruction during fall and spring quarters. It is designed as a week in which those interested in joining a fraternity may get a sense of each fraternity's values and what their members are like. Just as each individual interested in joining tries to find the best fit for themselves, so does each fraternity try to find the members that would best suit them.
